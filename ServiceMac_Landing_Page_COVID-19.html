COVID-19 Payment Assistance

We Care and Are Here to Help

The response to the Coronavirus is rapidly evolving. During this difficult time, ServiceMac is committed to supporting our customers with guidance on payment assistance options so that you can focus on the safety of yourself and your family.

If you are experiencing difficulty making your mortgage payment due to COVID-19, including:

  • Loss of employment
  • Reduction in income
  • Illness
  • Or other related COVID impacts preventing you from being able to make your monthly payment

Help Is Available

ServiceMac is taking the following steps to assist:

  • Payment Assistance: Offering the ability to temporarily suspend or reduce your monthly payment for three months (or more if needed) which is also known as a Forbearance plan.
  • Credit Reporting and Late Fees: We understand the importance of credit scores. ServiceMac will not report negative data to credit bureaus or assess late fees for customers actively performing on Forbearance plans (reduced or suspended payment agreements).
  • Foreclosures and Evictions: The initiation of new foreclosures, foreclosure sales and evictions are suspended through May 18, 2020.
